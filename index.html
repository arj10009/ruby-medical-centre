Ruby Medical Centre
Location

Parking & Facilities

Parallel street parking on Hampton Street, parking bay next to the Bakery (2 min walk), behind IGA, and Shire parking north (1 min walk). Wheelchair access patients can contact us for alternative options. Houses Pathology, Psychology, Audiology, Osteopathy, co-located with 777 Pharmacy.

Fees

Normal Hours Fees

Mon–Fri 8am–5pm. Standard Consultation $45/$90 · Long $60/$145 · Extended $75/$200 · Telehealth $30/$75 · Medical Certificate $10/$20 · Fitness to Drive $160 · Aviation $210 · Firearms $250 · Pre-Employment $160. Concession/Non-Medicare rates.

Fees

After Hours Fees

Mon–Fri before 8am/after 6pm, Sat before 8am/after 1pm, Sun/Public Holidays. Standard $103.80 (rebate $55.80) · Long $155.70 (rebate $95.70) · Extended $209.20 (rebate $134.20).

Fees

Consumables Fees

Biopsy $30 · Excision small $60, med $80, large $100-$120 · Flap repair $120 · Implanon $40-$60 · Mirena/IUD $40-$120 · Iron infusion $100 · Cryo $30 · ECG $30 · Ear syringing $30 · BP monitor $50 · Spirometry $20. Concession holders get 50% off.

Billing

Bulk Billing & Mission

Our motto is 'service to humanity'. Bulk billing for patients 16 and under and concession card holders. Payments on the day by cash or Eftpos. All fees at Doctor's discretion. 3 hours notice for cancellations. Missed appointments may incur a fee.
